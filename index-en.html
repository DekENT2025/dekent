Program Overview

• Degree Name : Bachelor of Science Entertainment Industry (B.Sc.)

• Bachelor's Degree – 4 years

• The program requires a total of 120 credits.

Admission Requirements

→ Applicants seeking admission to the first year of the bachelor's degree program must have successfully completed a Vocational Certificate (Por Wor Chor) in any field, or upper secondary education (Matthayom 6) in any academic track, or possess an equivalent qualification recognized by the Ministry of Education of Thailand or by the relevant educational authority in the applicant’s country of graduation.

→ For the transfer or continuing education program, individuals who have completed a Higher Vocational Certificate (Por Wor Sor) or an associate degree accredited by the Ministry of Education are eligible to complete the degree within 2 years. (Credit transfer is subject to the university’s regulations. Interested candidates may contact the program through its official communication channels for more information.)
